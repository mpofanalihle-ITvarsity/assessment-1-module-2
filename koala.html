ANIMALS MAP PLACES

KOALA

The koalas are the first pair of four donated to the South African public by Australia and the Taronga zoo in Sydney. The males arrived in South Africa on November 15. Two females are to join them in March next year when the Australian section in the zoo is officially opened.

Koalas may live from 13 to 18 years in the wild. While female koalas usually live this long, males may die sooner because of their more hazardous lives.
